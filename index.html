The market gap

Natural is easy to claim. Evidence is harder to see.

Natural cosmetic brands invest in ingredients, formulation, safety and testing. Yet most of this scientific work remains hidden inside technical documents, while consumers are presented with broad claims that are difficult to understand or compare.

The CNP solution

CNP creates a trust layer between scientific documentation and the purchase decision.

Scientific evidence is reviewed, structured and translated into a premium mark that consumers can recognize without needing to read a technical dossier.

1

Fragmented scientific evidence

Ingredient data, stability reports, safety assessments, efficacy studies and claims are stored in different technical documents.

2

Invisible product quality

Brands investing in stronger formulations and better testing struggle to make that difference visible at the shelf.

3

Low consumer comparability

Terms such as natural, clean, clinically tested and science-backed are often used without a clear common framework.

What CNP is

A scientific standard designed for real consumer decisions.

CNP independently reviews the evidence submitted by cosmetic brands and translates the results into a clear, traceable and premium consumer-facing mark.

Important: CNP does not replace cosmetic regulation. It provides an additional independent assessment of the scientific evidence, product documentation and claims submitted by the brand.

CNP Standard

What CNP evaluates.

The framework combines scientific rigor with accessible communication, so brands can document product quality and consumers can understand what the mark represents.

Natural ingredient verification

Origin, function, traceability and documentation of the relevant natural ingredients.

Active ingredient concentration

Assessment of whether selected active ingredients are present at a meaningful and documented concentration.

Formula quality and stability

Review of stability, compatibility, formulation quality and available supporting documentation.

In vitro and clinical evidence

Assessment of methods, study design, outcomes and relevance of the evidence to the product claims.

Claim substantiation

Evaluation of the alignment between consumer-facing claims and the evidence supporting them.

Scientific review

Independent expert oversight for advanced products, complex claims and higher assessment levels.

Built for credibility

Why brands should trust the CNP process.

Trust requires more than a premium visual identity. The value of CNP depends on traceable decisions, explicit limitations and a repeatable scientific framework.

Protocol

Structured assessment protocol

Every product is reviewed against a defined and version-controlled set of criteria.

Traceability

Evidence traceability

Each decision is connected to documented ingredients, studies, tests or technical records.

Independence

Independent scientific evaluation

The assessment focuses on evidence quality and relevance, not on promotional language.

Clarity

Transparent limitations

CNP distinguishes verified evidence, incomplete evidence and unsupported claims.

Testing

Qualified external testing

Where necessary, additional tests can be conducted through qualified independent laboratories.

Continuity

Annual reassessment

Material changes in formulation, suppliers, claims or evidence may require a focused reassessment.

The mark is not designed to say “trust us.”

It is designed to show consumers what they can trust—and why.
