The opportunity

Natural is easy to claim. Proof is harder to see.

Brands invest in ingredients, formulation and testing, yet the evidence remains buried in technical dossiers. Consumers face polished claims with no simple way to distinguish substantiated quality from marketing language.

The CNP solution

A standardized trust layer between scientific documentation and the purchase decision.

CNP does not replace cosmetic regulation. It independently assesses the evidence a brand chooses to submit and translates the result into a clear, traceable consumer-facing mark.

1

Fragmented evidence

Ingredient origin, concentration, stability, claim support and clinical data are stored across different documents.

2

Invisible scientific investment

Brands that conduct better testing often struggle to communicate the difference with precision and elegance.

3

Low comparability at the shelf

Consumers are asked to interpret broad terms such as natural, clean or clinically tested without a common framework.

CNP Standard v1.0

What CNP verifies.

The protocol is designed to evaluate the scientific substance behind a natural cosmetic product, while keeping the final communication clear enough for consumers and valuable enough for brands.

✦

Verified natural ingredients

Origin, function and documentation of the formulation’s relevant ingredients.

%

Minimum active concentration

Whether selected active ingredients are present at a meaningful documented level.

△

Formula stability

Review of stability, compatibility and quality documentation supplied by the brand.

⌁

In vitro and clinical evidence

Assessment of study design, methods, outcomes and the relevance of the evidence to product claims.

✓

Claim substantiation

Alignment between consumer-facing promises and the documentation supporting each claim.

◎

Scientific review board

Independent expert oversight for complex products, borderline claims or advanced certification levels.
